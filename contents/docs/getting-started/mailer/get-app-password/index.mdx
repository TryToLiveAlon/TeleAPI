## Guide

- **App password** - Here is a video which will [[**Guide**]](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4gE4f3Qq6K8) you how to create a **App password**.
- **Important Steps** - Some of the important steps are listed below go and follow them.

### Steps
<Stepper>
  <StepperItem title="Step 1: Enable 2-Step Verification">
      To generate an App Password in Gmail, you first need to enable **2-Step Verification** on your Google account. Go to [Google Account Security](https://myaccount.google.com/security) and under the "Signing in to Google" section, enable **2-Step Verification** if it is not already activated.
        </StepperItem>

          <StepperItem title="Step 2: Access App Passwords">
              Once 2-Step Verification is enabled, navigate to the [App Passwords](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords) page. If prompted, log in to your Google account again for security purposes.
                </StepperItem>

                  <StepperItem title="Step 3: Generate a New App Password">
                      In the **App Passwords** section, select the app and device you want to generate a password for. Since we are setting up email sending via **nodemailer**, choose **Mail** as the app and select **Other (Custom name)** for the device.
                        </StepperItem>

                          <StepperItem title="Step 4: Copy and Use the App Password">
                              Click the **Generate** button, and a 16-character password will be displayed. Copy this password and use it in your email-sending script as the authentication password instead of your actual Google account password.
                                </StepperItem>
                                </Stepper>

<Note type="success" title="Success">
